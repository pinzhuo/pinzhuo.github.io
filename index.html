About me

I received my Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in 2021, supervised by Prof. Gao Yang. I'm interested in machine learning. Recently, I mainly focus on how to use meta-learning or transfer learning to design efficient and robust supervised leanring or reinforcement learning algorithms. These algorithms are supposed to be applied into some real world applications.
