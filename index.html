About me

I am currently a Research Fellow in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Wen. I received my Ph.D. degree from the State Key Laboratory for Novel Software Technology at Nanjing University. My research interests include machine learning, representation learning, and related areas.
